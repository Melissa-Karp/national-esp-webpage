## National ESP Coordination Team
[cite_start]The increase in momentum across all six science centers to produce ESPs or ESP-like analysis inspired the creation of the National ESP initiative in 2022, followed by the formation of the National ESP Coordination Team in 2023[cite: 87].

> "And now we are going national with dedicated workshops to share ESP development across the country... We’ve developed the foundation. It’s malleable. It can be adapted."
> [cite_start]— *Kalei Shotwell (Alaska Fisheries Science Center)* [cite: 107, 108]

### Mission
[cite_start]To help launch, streamline, and coordinate the ESP process at all six science centers[cite: 89]. [cite_start]We enable a unified framework to integrate ecosystem and socioeconomic research within our stock assessment enterprise, providing an accounting mechanism to show progress toward ecosystem-based fisheries management (EBFM)[cite: 89].

### Scope of Activities & Deliverables
* [cite_start]**Web Maintenance:** Maintain this centralized space reviewing the ESP process and connecting reference data[cite: 93].
* [cite_start]**Step-by-Step Guides:** Develop and maintain a step-by-step guide for initiating and developing regional profiles[cite: 94].
* [cite_start]**National ESP Symposium:** Coordinate annual/biennial forums and workshops for cross-center sharing and innovation panels[cite: 96].
* [cite_start]**Training & Development:** Facilitate ongoing technical training ranging from brief seminars to multi-day co-creation hackathons[cite: 98].
* [cite_start]**Tracking Progress:** Track region status metrics, prioritized fish stocks, and real-world management use cases[cite: 100].
