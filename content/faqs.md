### How do ESPs relate to the Integrated Ecosystem Assessment (IEA) program?
ESPs are part of the larger, iterative IEA approach. They function as a tool to implement the IEA loop by translating large-scale indicators down to stock-specific applications.

### How do ESPs support the stock assessment program?
ESPs are directly responsive to the proposed 3-step process outlined in the *Implementing a Next Generation Stock Assessment Enterprise* framework (NG-SAIP; Lynch 2018). They facilitate more robust, accurate, and ecosystem-linked stock assessments.

### How do ESPs support EBFM?
They directly accelerate the adoption of Ecosystem-Based Fisheries Management by cleanly intersecting social science, ecological indicators, and actual management policy advice.

### How can ESPs support CEFI?
ESPs aid the operational production of climate-informed advice[cite: 121]. They serve as clear delivery pipelines bringing ocean and climate predictive outputs developed in CEFI directly into evaluation rooms.
