### How do ESPs relate to the Integrated Ecosystem Assessment (IEA) program?
[cite_start]ESPs are part of the larger, iterative IEA approach[cite: 113]. [cite_start]They function as a tool to implement the IEA loop by translating large-scale indicators down to stock-specific applications[cite: 113, 114].

### How do ESPs support the stock assessment program?
[cite_start]ESPs are directly responsive to the proposed 3-step process outlined in the *Implementing a Next Generation Stock Assessment Enterprise* framework (NG-SAIP; Lynch 2018)[cite: 116]. [cite_start]They facilitate more robust, accurate, and ecosystem-linked stock assessments[cite: 117].

### How do ESPs support EBFM?
[cite_start]They directly accelerate the adoption of Ecosystem-Based Fisheries Management by cleanly intersecting social science, ecological indicators, and actual management policy advice[cite: 119].

### How can ESPs support CEFI?
[cite_start]ESPs aid the operational production of climate-informed advice[cite: 121]. [cite_start]They serve as clear delivery pipelines bringing ocean and climate predictive outputs developed in CEFI directly into evaluation rooms[cite: 122].
